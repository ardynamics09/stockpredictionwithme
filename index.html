What is a Stock?

A stock represents ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you own a small part of that company.

Why do people invest in stocks?

People invest in stocks to grow their money over time and earn profits from price increases and dividends.

Types of Stocks

  • Growth Stocks – Companies expected to grow quickly.
  • Dividend Stocks – Companies that regularly pay profits to investors.
  • Blue Chip Stocks – Large and stable companies like Microsoft or Apple.
  • Penny Stocks – Very low price stocks with high risk.

Benefits of Investing

  • Wealth creation over long term
  • Beating inflation
  • Passive income through dividends
  • Ownership in companies

Risks in Stock Market

  • Market volatility
  • Economic changes
  • Company performance issues
  • Global events affecting markets
